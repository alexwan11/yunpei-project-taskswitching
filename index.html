Instructions

the left part is the shape task. the right part is the color task

Task Switching Experiment 1:

When the task starts, you will see either a shape or color on the screen.

If a shape appears, press the corresponding key based on the shape.

  • Press 'b' for circle.
  • Press 'n' for rectangle.

If a color appears, press the corresponding key based on the color.

  • Press 'b' for yellow.
  • Press 'n' for blue.

The experiment will consist of 50 tasks.

Try to respond as quickly and accurately as possible.


Task Switching Experiment 2:

This experiment is more difficult than Experiment 1.

  • Shape keys:
  • 'b' for circle
  • 'n' for rectangle
  • 'v' for triangle
  • 'm' for star
  • Color keys:
  • 'b' for yellow
  • 'n' for blue
  • 'v' for green
  • 'm' for red

The experiment will consist of 50 tasks.

Try to respond as quickly and accurately as possible.


Task Switching Experiment 3:

This experiment is similar to Experiment 2 but with a higher difficulty.

You will see two shapes and colors at a time.

Press the corresponding key based on the shape or color that appears on the top.

  • Shape keys:
  • 'b' for circle
  • 'n' for rectangle
  • 'v' for triangle
  • 'm' for star
  • Color keys:
  • 'b' for yellow
  • 'n' for blue
  • 'v' for green
  • 'm' for red

The shapes and colors in each pair will always be different.

The experiment will consist of 50 tasks.

Try to respond as quickly and accurately as possible.

Shape Task: Press 'b' for circle, 'n' for rectangle.

Color Task: Press 'b' for yellow, 'n' for blue.
